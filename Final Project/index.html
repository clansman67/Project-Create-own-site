Home in the mountain

The North Point, Nestled in the serene hills of Darjeeling, West Bengal, our institution stands as a timeless home of learning and character. Over the years, it has nurtured generations of remarkable individuals, including members of royal families and many distinguished personalities who have walked its corridors. For them, and for countless others, this school has been more than just a place of education—it has been a true home, shaping values, discipline, and lifelong memories.
